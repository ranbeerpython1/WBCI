Clientele

The valuation profession is a wide area of study and practice. Assignment parameters are also highly varied due to client problems and questions that need to be resolved. Traditional property appraisals for financial institutions which still makes up a large portion of appraisal requests has created branched questions from other areas of the economy that also need expertise in valuation analysis. Whether it is in consultancy or appraisal report preparation and presentation.

AXIA has 30 years of expertise driven by clients needing expertise that is required to solve valuation measurement issues. The market needs valuation specialists that can handle as many problems as possible AXIA has those resources. AXIA has partnerships with panels of experts to create we rounded analysis. From economics, to legal, to engineering and project planning; AXIA has that expertise on station to meet those needs. This allows our clients to embrace the challenges that come in owning, leasing and holding real estate.
